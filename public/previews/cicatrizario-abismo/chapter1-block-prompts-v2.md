# Capítulo 1 — Prompts V2 por Bloco

Todos os prompts importam obrigatoriamente:

1. `REN FIXED CHARACTER PROMPT` de `ren-fixed-prompt.md`.
2. `STYLE BIBLE` de `style-bible.md`.

Regra: nunca escrever só “same character”. Repetir sempre a descrição fixa do Ren dentro do prompt.

## Prompt base resumido embutido
Ren Avel: slim tired 20-year-old young man, olive-light skin, thin oval face, dark hazel exhausted almond eyes, messy medium black hair with uneven fringe over right eye, small diagonal scar on lower lip right side, narrow shoulders, lean underfed body, insecure posture. Earth outfit: black delivery jacket with blue reflective stripes and gray hoodie. Arvhal outfit: plain rough ash-gray tunic, visible copper F-0 tag pinned on chest, no jewelry, no armor, no heroic glow.
